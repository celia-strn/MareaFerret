WHY MARÉA

About Us

A family of travelers who found their haven

A FAMILY OF TRAVELERS

More than 30 countries
explored together

For over fifteen years, we have been traveling the world with our children. Different cultures, striking landscapes, precious memories.

Traveling has always been for us a way to open ourselves to the world, to slow down, to reconnect with what matters.

But above all, we learned what makes a place unforgettable: the feeling of being warmly welcomed, simplicity, authenticity, the soul of a place.

The day everything
stopped

Throughout our travels, we kept exploring...
And then one day, almost without looking for it, we discovered a paradise beach, just steps from home.

A special light.
Calm and clear water.
A place still untouched.

It was here that we felt what we had been searching for everywhere else.
A simple, beautiful, peaceful place.
A place we want to return to.

OUR PROJECT

Why Marea
was born

Maréa was born from this realization.
From the desire to share this little paradise with other travelers.

We didn't envision Maréa as just a seasonal rental, but as a living space, designed to welcome families, friends, curious travelers, and lovers of the Basin.

Every space has been designed as if we were hosting loved ones.
With attention, simplicity and care for detail.

Welcoming as
we love to travel

As travelers, we know what truly matters:

  • ✓ Arriving in a place where you feel expected
  • ✓ Finding your way easily
  • ✓ Having the right advice at the right time
  • ✓ Feeling free, without unnecessary constraints

A PLACE TO SLOW DOWN

At Maréa, you come to:

  • Unpack your bags
  • Walk barefoot
  • Enjoy the Basin beach
  • Live at the rhythm of the tides
  • Savor simple moments

Here, we don't rush.
We take our time.

Why the name
Maréa

Maréa evokes the tide, the natural movement of the Basin,
the permanent connection between land and ocean.

But also an invitation to live at the rhythm of nature,
to let the days flow gently.

It's a soft, luminous name that reflects who we are.
And it tells the story of this place better than any long speech.

We look forward
to welcoming you

If you've made it this far, perhaps you're looking for more than just accommodation.
A place where you truly feel good.

We would be delighted to welcome you to Maréa,
and to share with you this corner of the Basin that we love so much.

Maréa is not a destination.
It's a pause.
